5. Taniti's got good vibes, and violent crime is a rarity. But, like any growing tourist destination, keep an eye on your stuff. Reports of pickpocketing and other small-time mischief are on the rise.


6. Taniti knows how to celebrate! But heads up, on national holidays, some attractions and restaurants might be catching a break too. Plan accordingly, so you don't miss out on the island festivities.
