Island Harmony Transit: Navigating Taniti's Tropical Beauty

Most folks touch down in Taniti through the skies, catching the island vibes from above. If you're feeling a bit adventurous, there's a chance you might sail in on a cozy little cruise ship that drops anchor in Yellow Leaf Bay for a sweet one-night stay each week. Our quaint airport is equipped for small jets and propeller planes, ensuring a smooth landing for all our visitors. Exciting news on the horizon, we're currently sprucing up the airport, getting it ready to welcome bigger jets in the next few years. Taniti's sky is the limit!

Ground Transportation

Taniti's Easy-Going Ways of Getting Around: Hop on the friendly public buses that zip through Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily, making city travel a breeze. When you're ready to venture beyond the city limits, private buses have got you covered. Taxis are always there when you need them in Taniti City, and snagging a rental car from a local agency near the airport is a piece of cake. Feeling the island vibes? Rent a bike and helmet from the local vendors. With Taniti City's laid-back and walkable charm, many visitors choose to stay around Merriton Landing for an effortlessly enjoyable exploration on foot.
